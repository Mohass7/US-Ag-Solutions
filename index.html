Reclaim Your Soil. Restore Your Yield.

Innovative Salinity & Soil Health Solutions from US AG Solutions

Problem

One-third of the earth's soil is sodic.Sodic soil contains high levels of sodium ions, which disrupt soil structure, inhibit plant growth, andreduce agricultural productivity.

Solution

Soil Guard formulated to dislodge sodium, improve soil structure, and supply fast-acting calcium and nitrogen.

Meet Soil Guard

A new technology that is developed to treat your land of the common problem caused by salinity and high pH levels.

Salt Removal

Displacement Sodium(Na)

High soluble calcium replaces sodium on soll particles, flushes salt out of the root zone. and allows the root a full access to nutrients available

Soil Loosening

Improves Soil Structure
