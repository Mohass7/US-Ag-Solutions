Soil Guard

Formulated to dislodge sodium, improve soil structure, and supply fast-acting calcium and nitrogen.

Displaces Sodium(Na)
High soluble calcium replaces sodium on soil particles, flushes salt out of the root zone, and allows the root a full access to nutrients available.
Improvees Soil Structure
Calcium flucculates clay particles, opening up compated soil and improving dranage and acration.
Delivers Quick Nitrogen Uptake
10% total nitrogen fuels early growth, greening, and plant vigor.
Lowers Soil pH (Controlled)
Boosts Microbial Activity (Indirectly)
By improving structure and removing salt stress, microbial life rebounds.
Safe for Use with Most Crops
Gentle formulation allows for bread-spectrum application across vegetables, fruits, orchards, and row crops.

Phase A - Salt Removal

Dislodges sodium and chloride
Enhances water infiltration
Breaks up soil compaction
Initiates detoxification

Phase B - Soil Rebuilding

Replenishes nutrients
Introduces organics and carbon
Restores microbial activity
Boosts crop uptake and resistance

Apllication

Application Rates
Apply at 1 gallon per acre annually or as needed. For fields with severe salinity, an additional initial application may be necessary.
Flexible Timing and Methods
Suitable for once a year application on most crops. Compatible with drip foliar, flood and furrow irrigation systems.

Application Timing

Proper timnig of application is crucial for Soil Guard's performance. Apply early in the growing season, under suitable soil moisture conditions. For perennial or high-value crops, consider additional applications as necessary.
