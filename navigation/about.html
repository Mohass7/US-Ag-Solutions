We are Specialized in Soil Amendment Solutions

Who We Are

US AG Solutions is an agricultural innovation company dedicated to restoring soil health and increasing farm productivity through science-backed solutions. We specialize in addressing soil salinity, compaction, and nutrient inefficiencies with natural, effective treatments that work across diverse crop types and climates. Rooted in decades of agricultural experience and research, our team is passionate about helping farmers reclaim unproductive land, reduce input costs, and grow stronger, healthier crops.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with powerful, sustainable soil solutions that restore fertility, fight salinity, and increase profitability one acre at a time.

What We Stand For

We believe healthy soil is the foundation of successful agriculture. Our products are easy to apply, fast-acting, and built for real-world farming conditions. Every formula is backed by agronomy and field-proven across thousands of acres. Support that grows with you from the first treatment to long-term care. We're partners in your soil health journey.
